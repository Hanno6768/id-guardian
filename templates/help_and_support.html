Frequently Asked Questions
{# Registration #}

Registration & Verification

Go to the Register page and fill in your full legal name, date of birth, email, phone number, username, and 11-digit National ID. Upload a clear photo or scan of your identity document (JPG, PNG, or PDF — max 5 MB). After submitting, verify your email within 30 minutes. Your application then enters the reviewer queue.

After email verification your application enters the reviewer queue. A reviewer checks your information against your documents — this takes up to 5 business days. You receive an email once a decision is made: approval, rejection, or a correction request.

Check your spam folder first. If the email isn't there, go to the Check Your Inbox page and click Resend Verification Link. Each link is valid for 30 minutes. If it has expired, the page lets you request a new one automatically.

Yes. The rejection email includes the reviewer's specific reason. Address the issue and submit a new registration. There is no limit on reapplications, but submitting false information violates our Terms & Conditions and may be referred to national authorities.

A correction request means one detail needs fixing — for example your birthdate didn't match your document, or the uploaded file was unreadable. The email explains exactly what to fix. You do not start from scratch; simply re-register with the corrected information.
{# Login #}

Login & Password

When your application is approved you receive a Set Password link by email, valid for 24 hours. Click it, choose a secure password, and you are logged in automatically. If the link has expired, request a new one from the expired-link page.

Yes. The login form accepts either your username or your 11-digit National ID number in the identifier field.

Click Forgot Password? on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address. If you no longer have access to that email, contact support using the form below.
{# Documents #}

Documents & QR Codes

Passport, National ID, Driving Licence, Birth Certificate, Marriage Certificate, and Nationality Card. Upload files must be JPG, PNG, or PDF and must not exceed 5 MB.

Each verified document has a unique QR code. When scanned by an authorised third party it opens a SudaGuardian verification page confirming the document's authenticity. The QR encodes your document ID and user ID only — your National ID number is never exposed.

3–5 business days (Sunday–Thursday, 08:00–17:00 SST) per our SLA. If pending longer than 5 business days, contact support.
{# Security #}

Account & Security

No. Full name, username, National ID, date of birth, and gender are locked once verified. These are part of your permanent national identity record and can only change through an official governmental process.

No. Account deletion is permanently disabled. Your verified digital identity is a national record protected under the SudaGuardian data sovereignty framework. You may deactivate notifications and stop using the platform at any time.

Your National ID is encrypted with AES-256 before storage and is never held in plain text. Only authorised reviewers can access a decrypted version during review, and that access is logged. Passwords are hashed with a modern adaptive algorithm.

Go to My Profile → Active Sessions and click Log Out All Sessions immediately, then change your password. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact support below.
